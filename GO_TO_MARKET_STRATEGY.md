## Sales Strategy

### Sales Motion
1. **Initial Outreach** (LinkedIn, Cold Email)
   - CEO/CISO/Head of Security
   - Personalized message with use case relevance
   - Success metric: 15-20% response rate

2. **Discovery Call** (15 min)
   - Pain points: current SIEM, gaps, budget
   - Timeline: "When would you evaluate?"
   - Success metric: 40% of responses move forward

3. **Product Demo** (30 min)
   - Live system walkthrough
   - Risk assessment for their team (sample)
   - ROI calculator
   - Success metric: 60% want POC

4. **POC** (2-week trial)
   - Setup on 5-10 sample employees
   - Daily status check-ins
   - Success metric: 80% convert to paid

5. **Close** (1-month deal)
   - Standard contracts or NDAs
   - Onboarding plan
   - Success metric: Avg deal size $5K-15K ARR

### Sales Resources Needed
- Month 1-3: Founder-led sales
- Month 4-6: 1 full-time sales development rep
- Month 7+: Sales team scale (1 AE per $500K ARR)
